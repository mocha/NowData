Community Information Now System (CI:Now) is a community partnership of over 25 public, private and non-profit organizations that have worked together since 1998 to build a community network information system that provides:

  • Searchable, community resources Health and Human Services information
  • Culturally relevant information
  • Rural and Urban information about the Alamo Area
  • In both Spanish and English
  • Serving both community users and social service professionals
